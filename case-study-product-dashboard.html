The Challenge

DigitalOcean's rapidly expanding product portfolio spanning 35+ product lines across AI/ML, Compute, Databases, Storage, and Networking created significant information gaps for customer-facing teams.

  • Fragmented product knowledge across multiple teams
  • Inconsistent customer conversations and messaging
  • Missed opportunities to demonstrate full capabilities
  • Excessive time searching for accurate information

The Solution

Designed and built a comprehensive Product Intelligence Dashboard consolidating product information, competitive analysis, and roadmap visibility into a single, intuitive platform.

  • Complete product portfolio documentation
  • Feature matrices and technical specifications
  • Competitive positioning vs AWS, Azure, GCP
  • Real-time availability and compliance status
  • Customer use cases and architecture patterns

Impact & Results

20% Enablement Time Reduction

Sales and CS teams reduced product research time, enabling more customer-focused activities.

100% Team Adoption

Across TAM, CSM, and Sales teams with immediate adoption due to clear value.
